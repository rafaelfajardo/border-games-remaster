Crosser and La Migra are a product of SWEAT, a loose collaborative dedicated to the creation of socially conscious games.

The members of SWEAT that have contributed to Crosser and La Migra are: Francisco Ortega-Grimaldo, Carmen Escobar, Miguel Tarango, Marco Ortega, Ryan Molloy, Tomás Márquez-Carmona, Scott Leutenegger, Chris GauthierDickey, and Rafael Fajardo.
